Agile in Education and Training

In May 2018, a group of agile practitioners and educators gathered at the XP2018 conference in Porto to discuss techniques and experiences in education people (students, colleagues, trainees, etc) about agile practices. The workshop was organised by Robert Chatley and Craig Anslow. The outputs of the day are presented below:
